Simple Steps to a Zero-Waste Period

  • Switch to Reusables

    A menstrual cup, disc, or period underwear can replace thousands of disposables over their lifetime, saving you money and saving the planet from waste.

  • Choose Plastic-Free Disposables

    If you're not ready for reusables, look for tampons without plastic applicators and pads made from organic, biodegradable materials like cotton and bamboo.

  • Advocate and Educate

    Talk to friends and family about sustainable options. The more people who make the switch, the bigger our collective impact becomes.

  • Rethink Your Period Care Routine

    Opt for reusable cloth wipes instead of disposable ones, and store your products in a washable cloth bag instead of a single-use plastic one.
