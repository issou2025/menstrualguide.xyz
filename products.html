Choosing the Right Product

There's no one-size-fits-all solution when it comes to menstrual products. Your choice depends on your flow, lifestyle, comfort preferences, and values.

Types of Menstrual Products

Sanitary Pads

Absorbent materials worn in underwear to collect menstrual flow externally.

✅ Pros
  • Easy to use
  • No insertion required
  • Various sizes available
  • Good for beginners
❌ Cons
  • Can feel bulky
  • Visible under tight clothing
  • Not suitable for swimming
  • May cause odor
Best for: Overnight use, heavy flow days, beginners

Tampons

Cylindrical absorbent materials inserted into the vagina to absorb menstrual flow internally.

✅ Pros
  • Discreet and comfortable
  • Good for active lifestyles
  • Can swim while wearing
  • No external bulk
❌ Cons
  • Learning curve for insertion
  • Risk of TSS if left too long
  • May cause dryness
  • Not eco-friendly
Best for: Active days, swimming, sports

Menstrual Cups

Reusable silicone or rubber cups that collect menstrual flow internally.

✅ Pros
  • Eco-friendly & cost-effective
  • 12-hour wear time
  • No dryness or odor
  • Can last 5-10 years
❌ Cons
  • Steep learning curve
  • Initial cost is high
  • Requires cleaning
  • May be messy to empty
Best for: Eco-conscious users, long-term savings

Menstrual Discs

Flexible discs that sit at the vaginal fornix to collect flow, allowing for mess-free period sex.

✅ Pros
  • Can wear during sex
  • 12-hour wear time
  • Higher capacity
  • Less suction than cups
❌ Cons
  • Can be tricky to insert
  • May leak if not positioned right
  • Single-use options costly
  • Limited availability
Best for: Those wanting intimacy during periods

Period Underwear

Specially designed underwear with built-in absorbent layers to replace or supplement other products.

✅ Pros
  • Comfortable like regular underwear
  • Reusable & eco-friendly
  • Great backup protection
  • No insertion required
❌ Cons
  • High upfront cost
  • Need multiple pairs
  • Requires washing
  • May feel wet
Best for: Light days, backup protection, sleeping

Reusable Cloth Pads

Washable fabric pads that snap onto underwear, offering an eco-friendly alternative to disposables.

✅ Pros
  • Eco-friendly
  • Soft & comfortable
  • Cost-effective long-term
  • No chemicals
❌ Cons
  • Requires washing
  • Initial investment
  • Bulkier when traveling
  • May stain
Best for: Home use, sensitive skin, eco-conscious users

Tips for Choosing Your Product

🌊

Consider Your Flow

Heavy flow? Look for high-absorbency options or combine products. Light flow? You have more flexibility in choices.

🏃‍♀️

Think About Lifestyle

Active? Internal products offer more freedom. Prefer comfort? External options might be better.

🌍

Environmental Impact

Reusable products significantly reduce waste and save money long-term, though require more maintenance.
